I'm not really a car guy, but I love seeing old cars around town even if they aren't running. New cars suck for many reasons. To name a few...touch buttons, warning messages, obnoxiously bright headlights, too many annoying beeps, too many annoying warning messages, auto brights, automatic start/stop, subscription features, etc. etc. 

My first few cars were from the early 90s with manual transmissions. I really miss that, the feeling of actually interacting with the car while driving. Today driving is just...uninspiring.
